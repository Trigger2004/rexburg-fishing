Key regulations

Teton River (above canyon) — closed Jun 1–30. Season Jul 1–Nov 30.
Sand Creek Ponds 3 & 4 — closed Jan 1–Jul 1 for waterfowl nesting.
Cutthroat trout — mandatory C&R on Teton + South Fork Snake. Red slash under jaw.
Sturgeon — C&R only. Cannot be removed from water. Barbless hooks required.
Henry's Lake — artificial lures and flies only. No bait. 3-trout limit.
Live bait — no live fish, leeches, frogs, or salamanders. Live crayfish OK if caught on-site.
High-harvest — Brook trout 25/day · Whitefish 25/day · Walleye unlimited.
