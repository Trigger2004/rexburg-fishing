Key regulations

Teton River (above canyon) — closed June 1–30 for cutthroat spawning. Season runs July 1–Nov 30.
Sand Creek Ponds 3 & 4 — closed Jan 1–July 1 for waterfowl nesting. Non-motorized boats allowed July 16–Apr 15.
Sand Creek WMA (winter) — closed Jan 1–Apr 1 (some areas May 1) for wintering big game.
Yellowstone Cutthroat — mandatory catch-and-release on Teton River and South Fork Snake. ID by red/orange slash marks under jaw.
Sturgeon — catch-and-release only. Must not be removed from water. Barbless hooks + breakaway leader required.
Live bait ban — no live fish, leeches, frogs, salamanders, waterdogs, or shrimp. Exception: crayfish caught on-site.
High-limit species — Brook Trout: 25/day. Mountain Whitefish: 25/day. Walleye: no limits (harvest encouraged).
